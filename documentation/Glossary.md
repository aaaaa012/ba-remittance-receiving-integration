# Glossary of Terms

| Term | Definition |
|:---|:---|
| **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)** | Laws, regulations, and procedures intended to prevent criminals from disguising illegally obtained funds as legitimate income. |
| **Beneficiary** | The ultimate recipient of the funds (also known as "Receiver"). |
| **Idempotency** | A property of an operation whereby it can be applied multiple times without changing the result beyond the initial application (e.g., paying the same transaction twice). |
| **KYC (Know Your Customer)** | The process of verifying the identity of a client. |
| **MTO (Money Transfer Operator)** | A financial service provider that offers cross-border money transfer services. |
| **Payout** | The act of disbursing funds to the beneficiary's bank account or wallet. |
| **PEP (Politically Exposed Person)** | An individual who is or has been entrusted with a prominent public function, presenting a higher risk for potential involvement in bribery and corruption. |
| **Settlement** | The actual transfer of funds between financial institutions to discharge obligations. |
| **STP (Straight-Through Processing)** | An automated process done purely through electronic transfers with no manual intervention. |
